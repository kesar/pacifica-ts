We are excited for you to start trading on Pacifica. Follow these steps below to get started quickly and securely.

### 

[](#what-do-i-need-to-trade-on-hyperliquid)

Getting Started Overview

1.  [Connect & Fund Your Wallet](/testnet/how-to-start-trading/connect-and-fund-your-wallet)
    
    1.  Set up a supported wallet, deposit collateral, and securely connect it to Pacifica.
        
    
2.  Choose Your Trading Method
    
    1.  [Desktop](/testnet/how-to-start-trading/trading-via-desktop): Learn how to place trades and manage positions using our web-based desktop interface.
        
    2.  [Mobile](/testnet/how-to-start-trading/trading-via-mobile): Trade on-the-go using your smartphone with our intuitive mobile interface.
        
    3.  [API](/testnet/how-to-start-trading/trading-via-api): Integrate programmatic trading strategies through our robust API.
        
    
3.  Start Trading
    
    1.  After connecting your wallet and choosing your method, you’re ready to place trades, manage positions, and explore Pacifica’s trading features. Read our [trading overview here.](/trading-on-pacifica/overview)
        
    

* * *

If you’re new to perpetual futures, we recommend exploring the [Trading on Pacifica](/trading-on-pacifica/overview) section to learn more about order types, margin, leverage, funding rates, and risk management.
